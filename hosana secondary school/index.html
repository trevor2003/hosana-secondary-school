Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures academic excellence, moral values, and leadership skills in our students, preparing them for success in higher education and life.

Our Vision

To be a leading secondary school recognized for academic excellence, character development, and innovative teaching that transforms students into responsible global citizens.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Innovation, and Community Service guide everything we do at Hosanna Secondary School.

School Motto: "Strive for Excellence"

At Hosanna Secondary School, we believe every student has the potential to achieve greatness. Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and comprehensive curriculum create an environment where students can discover their talents and reach their full potential.

Academic Excellence

Our rigorous academic programs prepare students for success in national examinations and higher education. We maintain high standards while supporting each student's individual learning journey.

Character Development

We emphasize moral values, leadership skills, and social responsibility. Our students graduate as well-rounded individuals ready to contribute positively to society.

Modern Facilities

Our campus features well-equipped classrooms, science laboratories, computer lab, library, sports facilities, and residential accommodation for boarding students.

Admissions Information

Welcome to Hosanna Secondary School! We are excited to help you begin your journey toward academic excellence.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) certificate
  • Birth certificate or sworn affidavit
  • Medical report from a recognized health facility
  • Two passport-size photographs
  • Transfer certificate (for students from other schools)

  5. Fee Payment: Pay required fees to secure placement
  6. Orientation: Attend orientation session before term begins
